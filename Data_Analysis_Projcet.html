Titanic Data Analysis¶

This is titanic survival dataset which have information about the people who travelled in the titanic.This dataset contains information about 891 passengers having different parameters and with the information they survived or not. the dataset contains variuos columns which are as under

Dataset Parameters:¶

  • Survival : Survival (0 = Not Survived ,1 = Survived)
  • Pclass : Passenger class (1 = Upper class ,2 = Middle class , 3 = Lower class)
  • Name : Name of the passenger
  • Sex : Male/Female
  • Age : Age of the Passenger

  • Sibsp : No of siblings /spouses aboard the Titanic

        1. sibling = brother,sister,stepbrother, stepsister
    2. spouses = husband ,wife

  • Parch : No of parents and children aboard the Titanic

        1. Parent  = mother and father
    2. Child = daughter, son
    3. some children travelled alone or with nanny so they have parch = 0 for them
  • Ticket : Ticket no of passenger
  • Fare : Fare of the ticket
  • Cabin : Contains the cabin number
  • Embarked : Contains port of Embarkation. Contains three value.(C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton)

Questions To Explore:¶

  • How many people survived and how many not? Did Gender played any important role?
  • Did Passenger Class Played any role ?
  • Did any particular age grouped people survived more?
  • Did female and child of particular class got more priority?
  • Travelling from some port for (2 = Middle class or 3 = lowe class) affect their survival rate?
  • Did family relation played any role ? ( Whole family survived or something like no one from whole family survived)

Conclusion¶

From the above analysis we can conclude the following

  • From data exploration by gender it is found that females were given higher priority as their survival rate(68.13%) quite higher than male survival rate(31.87%).

  • Survival rate of class 1 and 2 is higher than class 3 which is only (24.24%).Although females from class 3 were also given higher priority but not as compared to class 1 & 2.The survival rate of females from higher class is almost (90 to 96)% but for class 3 it is only 50% concluded from pclass exploration.

  • Exploring by Age data it is found that if family didnt get a chance to survive than whole family dies and if in the case family get a chance to survive than children and female members were given priority.

  • Exploration from port of embarkation it is found that males from Port Q were mostly died except 3.May be the case that embarktion from particular port were given particular staying area and from there survival is almost impossible.But in the case of females of any port of embarkation survival rate is simmilar.

  • It is also found that children who were not able to survive were from class 3.

  • Because few limitation of the dataset like we are only having 204 values for the cabin column we are not able to identify survival rate of particular cabin group.May be the case that the people from particular cabin group were not able to survive as they got highly damaged,we can not ignore such type of cases.

  • Also the information of the crew members (who were working in the Titanic)were not given in the dataset,so we are unable to find out that if the families of the crew members were given higher priorities or not?
